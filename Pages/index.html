Episode VIII

THE LAST JEDI

In the vast galaxy of education, where knowledge is the force that binds the student body together, a young coding prodigy named Sugee emerged. Born on October 25, 2025, little did Sugee know that destiny had a digital quest in store.

On the 30th of November 2023, the school of Fontys approached Sugee with a monumental challenge to wield the power of code and create a website that would transcend the ordinary. The echoes of this request rippled through the hallowed halls, stirring a sense of anticipation among students and educators alike.

As the binary stars align and the pixels come to life, Fontys stands on the brink of a digital revolution. The school, unaware of the marvels that await, is about to embark on an intergalactic journey of innovation and creativity.

The force is strong with Sugee, and the website will soon unveil the magic of coding, the prowess of imagination, and the boundless possibilities of the digital realm. The school, like a starship ready for warp speed, is set to navigate uncharted territories in the vast universe of technology.

Will Fontys ascend to become a legendary institution, a beacon of technological marvel? Only time will reveal the chapters of this epic saga an odyssey where bytes and bits become the building blocks of the future.
